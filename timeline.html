647 A.D. - 1200 A.D.

Rajputs

Rajputana, also known as Rajput country, was a region in northwestern India that was home to the Rajput people, a Hindu warrior caste. The region was known for its rich cultural heritage, martial traditions, and impressive forts and palaces.

1206 A.D. - 1526 A.D.

Delhi Sultanate

The Delhi Sultanate was a Muslim kingdom that ruled over parts of India from the 13th to the 16th century. It was known for its military conquests, art and architecture, and cultural contributions to the Indian subcontinent.

1526 A.D. - 1761 A.D.

Mughal Empire

The Mughal Empire was a powerful Islamic state that ruled over most of the Indian subcontinent from the early 16th to the mid-19th century. It was known for its rich cultural and artistic achievements, as well as its military prowess
